Traffic Perception

Our primary focus remains to be pedestrian safety and traffic violations in this project. Specifically, pedestrian warning system alongside traffic light jumping are important areas of focus, which have applications both in autonomous vehicles and automated law enforcement. The present systems rarely have little, if any deep learning integrations to catch violators and prevent harm to pedestrians. Our project aims to detect these facts in a fast, efficient, and effective manner.

About 5%ers

Our project, "Traffic Perception," focuses on pedestrian safety and traffic violations. Using deep learning models like YOLOv8 and OpenCV, we aim to enhance automated law enforcement and prevent road mishaps efficiently.
